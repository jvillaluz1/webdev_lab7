Interesting Facts

  • Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun.
  • Venus is the hottest planet with surface temperatures that can melt lead.
  • Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet and has a Great Red Spot - a storm that has been raging for hundreds of years.
